Core opportunity

The maid service that actually shows up.

Most competitors say clean, trusted, insured, and guaranteed. MoreHands has a sharper claim: a no-cancel promise backed by a $100 guarantee.

Executive readout

What competitors are doing

Seasonal urgency Houston competitors are actively running spring-clean messaging with "book now" calls to action.
Free-time framing The most repeated emotional promise is not cleanliness. It is getting weekends, calm, and family time back.
Quote friction Free estimate, book now, and instant price claims appear across the category.
Suburb targeting Molly Maid is running variants for Cypress, Katy, Pearland, Brenham, Sealy, and more.

Recommended MoreHands campaign

What to test first

01

Reliability Promise

Lead with "Your maid service should not cancel on you." Pair it with the $100 no-cancel guarantee and instant quote CTA.

02

Spring Clean Without Losing The Weekend

Directly counter active Houston spring-clean ads with MoreHands' sharper reliability and booking convenience.

03

Suburb Landing Variants

Create Katy, Cypress, Spring, The Woodlands, Pearland, and Heights variants. Competitors are already localizing the same creative.

04

Marketplace Counter

Homeaglow owns the $19 intro. MoreHands should own trust: trained teams, no-cancel guarantee, consistent service, and quality control.
